Mexican Strawberry Water (Agua de Fresa)

Description:

Mexican Strawberry Water, also know as Agua de Fresa, is a refreshing and delicious drink that is popular throughout Mexico. The drink is ade by blending fresh strawberries with water and sugar until a smooth mixture is formed. The mixture is then strained to remove any seeds or pulp, and served over ice. The result is a sweet and fruity drink that is perfect for hot summer days.

Agua de Fresa is a simple and easy-to-make drink that can be enjoyed any time of the year. In Mexico it is a popularly served at parties and other gatherings. The drink can be made with fresh or frozen strawberries, and can be adjusted to suit your tatse preferences. Some people like to add a splash of lime juice or a few sprigs of fresh mint to their Agua de Fresa for an extra burst of flavor.
